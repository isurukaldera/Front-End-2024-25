How To get a refund for broken items during shipping ?

Contact the seller and/or the shipping carrier, document the damage, and potentially file a claim with the carrier if the package was insured. The seller may offer a refund, replacement, or repair, while the carrier will likely compensate you if a claim is valid.

How to Track Your Order?

After placing an order, check your email or account dashboard for a tracking number. Use this number on the shipping carrier’s website to monitor delivery status. If no tracking information is available, contact the seller for an update.

How to Return an Item?

Log in to your account, go to your order history, and select the item you wish to return. Follow the return instructions, including repacking the item and using the provided return label. Once the seller receives and verifies the item, a refund or exchange will be processed.

How to Cancel an Order?

Go to your account’s order history and select the order you want to cancel. If the order hasn’t been shipped yet, you’ll see a Cancel option. If it’s already in transit, you’ll need to wait for delivery and then request a return. For urgent cancellations, contact customer support directly.
